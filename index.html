A Cover Worth 1000 Words


how aesthetic features can influence your purchases

Aesthetics tend to be taken for granted. Who cares if something looks good? Function before style, right? While there is some truth to that, visual appeal definitely plays an important role in our everyday lives. Aesthetics can affect everything from how we feel, to the decisions that we make - sometimes without us even realizing it!
Often when we think ‘aesthetics’, the first things that come to mind are probably envy-enticing photos that flood Instagram and Tumblr. However, aesthetic is used in the creation of books, movies, and even music, and is an overarching term that includes everything from typography to delivery. Arguably, it’s these more basic building blocks that can have the biggest influence on the human psyche. Whether you’re a customer or a product designer, learning more about aesthetics can really help you to make savvier decisions. Not convinced? Understandable - it can be a tough concept to wrap your head around, and your first instinct might be to click out of this page entirely. Let’s see if we can change your mind, though, by starting with a quick example.
